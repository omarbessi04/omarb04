Asian Kung-Fu Generation (AKFG) is a Japanese alternative rock band from Yokohama, active since 1996. They blend post-punk energy with melodic hooks and introspective lyrics, they're also my favorite band!

This is a personal data-visualization experiment; part language learning, part data analysis. I translate their Japanese lyrics by hand:

  1. Write down lyrics, highlight unknown words, and break them apart. Example.
  2. Run a script to extract features: track length, unique character count, etc.
  3. Find interesting patterns in the data.
