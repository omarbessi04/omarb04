Neural Architecture Search Research

Worked over the summer of 2025 in Reykjavík University researching how to optimize CNN models for sleepstage classification.
We called it SLEAP (Sleep Labeling using Evolutionary Algorithms and Pytorch).
I worked on designing and implementing a genetic algorithm that would identify good kernel sizes for the models.
Turns out only tuning kernel sizes didn't do much, but that's a story for a different time.

The final paper is still being worked on, but when it's ready I'll add it here.
